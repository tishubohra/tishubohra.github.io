HISTORY AND ARCHITECTURE

Indian architecture is a synthesis of indigenous styles and external influences which has lent it a unique characteristic of its own. In the post Harappan period architectural styles have been classified as Hindu, Buddhist and Jain, The medieval period saw the synthesis of Persian and indigenous styles of architecture. Thereafter the colonial period brought the influence of Western architectural forms to India
